# ♟️ Mirror Learner AI

🚀 A Chess AI that doesn't just play — it **learns how YOU think**

---

## 🧠 Concept

Most chess engines focus on playing the strongest move.

**Mirror Learner AI** follows a different philosophy:

> Instead of defeating the player, it tries to understand and mirror the player's thinking style.

---

## 🔥 Features

* 🧬 **Style DNA System**

  * Detects aggressive / defensive playstyle

* ♟️ **Opening Memory**

  * Learns and remembers opening patterns

* ⚠️ **Blunder DNA**

  * Tracks repeated mistakes and simulates human-like errors

* 🧠 **Neural Bias (WIP)**

  * AI decision influenced by learned behavior

* 🎮 **Interactive GUI**

  * Built with Pygame (mouse-based chess)
